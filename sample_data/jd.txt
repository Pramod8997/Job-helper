Senior Backend Engineer

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our platform team. You'll
be designing and building scalable microservices that power our core product.

Must Have:
- 3+ years of Python development experience
- Experience with PostgreSQL or similar relational databases
- Docker and container orchestration (Kubernetes a plus)
- REST API design and implementation
- CI/CD pipeline experience
- Strong understanding of distributed systems

Nice to Have:
- Experience with Redis or similar caching solutions
- Message queue systems (RabbitMQ, Kafka)
- Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Monitoring and observability (Prometheus, Grafana)

Soft Skills:
- Strong communication and mentoring abilities
- Ownership mindset — you take responsibility for your services end-to-end
- Comfortable working in a fast-paced, collaborative environment

We offer competitive compensation, flexible work arrangements, and a culture
that values continuous learning. We are an equal opportunity employer.
